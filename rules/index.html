General Rules

  1. Respect all players and Policy Enforcement Team members. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.
  2. No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics.
  3. Do not share or use another player's account.
  4. No advertising other servers, services, or social media accounts without prior permission from the Management Team
  5. Keep chat appropriate for all ages. No NSFW content.
  6. Do not impersonate Policy Enforcement Team members, Developers, Management Members, or other players. Developers, P.E.T members, and Management Team members will have a special nametag above their head
  7. No spamming in chat/chat walling/flooding chat.
  8. Do not attempt to crash or lag the server.
  9. Follow instructions and decisions from Policy Enforcement Team members and mebers of the Management Team.
  10. Any behavior that harms the community may result in punishment.
  11. Do not attempt to DDoS, DoS, Stress Test, "Boot" players, Policy Enforcement Team Members, Developers, or Management Team Members
  12. No racism, discrimination, hommophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, furryphobia, sexism, or other forms of harassment or language intended to insult, hurt, offend, or demean a user of the internet or a member of True-Fort Surival Minecraft Multiplayer and Factions

PVP Rules

  1. PVP is disabled in spawns world
  2. No spawn killing or camping.
  3. Combat logging (leaving to avoid PVP) is strictly prohibited.
  4. No using alternative accounts to gain PVP advantages.
  5. No exploiting PVP mechanics or hitboxes.
  6. PVP-related glitches must be reported, not exploited.
  7. Excessive toxicity is not permitted
  8. No exploiting known or unknown bugs or glitches with the server or minecraft as a whole.

Building Rules

  1. No offensive or inappropriate builds.
  2. Do not build within 100 blocks of another player's base without permission.
  3. No floating trees or excessive pillar builds.
  4. builds must not be excessive on the server infrastructure or network.
  5. No inappropriate redstone builds

Communication Rules

  1. English is the primary language for public chats.
  2. No excessive caps or meaningless spam.
  3. Keep political and religious discussions out of public chats.
  4. Do not share personal information (yours or others').
  5. No discussion of illegal activities.
  6. Voice chat must remain appropriate for all ages.
  7. Respect all players regardless of skill level.
  8. No backseat moderating or mini-modding - report issues to staff.
  9. Do not argue with staff decisions in public chats.
  10. No racism, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, sexism, furryphobia in public chats
