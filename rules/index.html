Server Rules & Regulations

Last Updated: June 20, 2025

Violations may result in temporary or permanent bans. All rules apply to both in-game and Discord server.

General Rules

  1. 1. Respect all players and staff members. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated. Zero Tolerance
  2. 2. No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics. Permanent Ban
  3. 3. Do not share or use another player's account. 30-day Ban
  4. 4. No advertising other servers, services, or social media accounts without prior permission. 7-day Ban
  5. 5. Keep chat appropriate for all ages. No NSFW content. Permanent Ban
  6. 6. Do not impersonate staff members or other players. 14-day Ban
  7. 7. No spamming in chat/chat walling/flooding chat. 24 hour Mute
  8. 8. Do not attempt to crash or lag the server. Permanent Ban
  9. 9. Follow instructions and decisions from staff members. Varies
  10. 10. Any behavior that harms the community may result in punishment. Varies
  11. 11. Do not attempt to DDoS, DoS, or stress test the server. Permanent Ban + Report to the FBI Cyber Crime Tip Line
  12. 12. No racism, discrimination, homophobia, transphobia, xenophobia, furryphobia, or sexism. Permanent Ban
  13. 13. No disclosing P.I.I of any person publicly or privately (Personally Identifiable Information) / No DoX attacts Permanent Ban

PVP Rules

  1. 14. PVP is disabled in spawn world. N/A. Impossible to violate
  2. 15. No spawn killing or camping. 3-day Ban
  3. 16. Combat logging (leaving to avoid PVP) is strictly prohibited. 7-day Ban
  4. 17. No using alternative accounts to gain PVP advantages. 30-day Ban
  5. 18. No exploiting PVP mechanics or hitboxes. 14-day Ban
  6. 19. PVP-related glitches must be reported, not exploited. 90 Day Ban (does not double with last rule of this category)
  7. 20. Excessive toxicity is not permitted. 12 Hour Mute
  8. 21 No exploiting known or unknown bugs or glitches within the server or within minecraft as a whole. 90-day Ban

Building Rules

  1. 22. No offensive or inappropriate builds. Permanent Ban
  2. 23. Do not build within 100 blocks of another player's base without permission. Warning
  3. 24. No floating trees or excessive pillar builds. Warning
  4. 25. Builds must not strain server performance. Removal
  5. 26. No inappropriate redstone builds. Removal

Communication Rules

  1. 27. English is the primary language for public chats. Warning
  2. 28. No excessive caps or meaningless spam. 1-hour Mute
  3. 29. Keep political and religious discussions out of public chats. 1-day Mute
  4. 30. Do not share personal information (yours or others'). 14-day Ban
  5. 31. No discussion of illegal activities. Permanent Ban
  6. 32. Voice chat must remain appropriate for all ages. Permanent Ban
  7. 33. Respect all players regardless of skill level. Warning
  8. 34. No backseat moderating - report issues to staff. 1-hour Mute
  9. 35. Do not argue with staff decisions in public chats. 1-day Mute
  10. 36. No hate speech of any kind in public chats. Permanent Ban
